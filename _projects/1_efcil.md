We study how visual models can incrementally learn new classes without storing raw exemplars from previous tasks. This project investigates the hidden imbalance between current data and prototype-based memory, and develops prototype-aware sampling and adaptive class-balanced objectives for long-sequence continual learning.

This line of work is connected to our CVPR 2026 Findings paper on exemplar-free class-incremental learning.
